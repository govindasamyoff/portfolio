Interesting fact about the Sun

Sun was formed about 1000 crore years back. There were four yugas with each consisting of few lakhs years. Krita Yuga, Treta Yuga, Dwapara Yuga, Kali Yuga. After each yuga, there is a deluge, and the next yuga comes into existence. In one of the yugas, the brahma, the creator of whole universe disappeared and when he came back in the next yuga, he wanted to know about happenings in earlier yuga and he approached Lord Shiva for the purpose...
